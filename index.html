about

I am a planerary science student studying Mars and Venus. I also serve as the Director of Advocacy for ASU's Graduate and Professional Student Association. I am also a member of the Space Governance Lab and a researcher on a new project working to advance diversity and inclusion in space.

I have a BA in Physics from Wheaton College Massachusetts and am now pursuing a PhD in Geological Sciences from Arizona State University.

I am primarily interested in volcanism throughout our solar system. I have studied cryovolcanism on Charon and steep-sided domes on Venus. I am also interested in how Earth analog fieldwork can assist us in understanding our planetary neighbors.
